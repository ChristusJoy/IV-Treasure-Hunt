Welcome to the Treasure Hunt Adventure!

How to Play

  1. Click on the "Start Hunt" button below to view the treasure map.
  2. On the map, you'll see numbered buttons from 1 to 11.
  3. Click on these buttons to discover hidden stickers.
  4. For spots 1-9, you'll see images of the locations.
  5. Spots 10 and 11 are bonus locations with no images.
  6. Use the "Back to Map" button to return to the main map at any time.
  7. Collect the most number of sticker corresponding to your team to win the huny

Are you ready to begin your adventure?
